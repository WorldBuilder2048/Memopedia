About Memopedia

Memopedia is a free encyclopedia dedicated to documenting the laws, anomalies, and unexplained phenomena of everyday life.

Unlike traditional encyclopedias, Memopedia focuses on events that are widely experienced but rarely explained, such as disappearing objects, tangled wires, and unexpected device failures.

Memopedia treats absurdity with seriousness, describing impossible or irrational events as if they were part of a consistent system.

All articles are interconnected and may describe different aspects of the same underlying structure of anomalies.
